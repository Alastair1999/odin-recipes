BBQ Ribs

Description

These ribs are very yummy, sticky and easy to stove

There is only 7 key ingredients. The ribs can be served with a variety of side dishes such as rice or mashed potatoes.

Receip

  • 1/4 cup steak sauce
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 4 (6 ounce) country style pork ribs/li>

Steps

  1. Whisk together steak sauce, ketchup, worcestershire sauce, garlic and water until smooth
  2. Place ribs in a saucepan with a lid and pour the sauce on top. Simmer the ribs for 45 minutes / until tender at a medium heat
