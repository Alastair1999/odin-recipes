Dumplings

Description

Dumplings are a quick snack to make and only require 6 ingredients. The entire preparation and cooking process only takes 20 minutes

With this receipe there are 6 servings. These particular dumplings are chicken dumplings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 table spoon margarine
  • 1/2 cup milk

Steps

  1. Stir together flour, baking powder, sugar and salt. Add milk to soften the dough and cut in butter until crumbly.
  2. Drop spoonfuls into boiling stew. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes then serve.
  3. Optional - add parsley to make parsley dumplings. Add 1 tablespoon of parsley flakes
