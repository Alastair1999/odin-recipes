Crepes

Description

This is a simple 3 step receipe for crepes. You only need 6 ingredients and the cooking time is only 2 minutes

Crepes are versatile and can be enjoyed with a variety of toppings. The above photo has creeps with fruit and sugar. Other combinations with nutella and banana are also very common and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. Whisk together the flour and the eggs. Gradually add milk and water. Then add salt and butter.
  2. Heat a fying pan (medium heat) and pour the batter in, 1/4 cup for each crepe.
  3. Cook for 2 minutes until bottom is brown and serve it hot.
